Knitwear refers to any type of clothing that has been knitted, which involves using needles and yarn to create fabric. The process of knitting results in a flexible, stretchy material that is ideal for creating cozy and functional garments. From sweaters to scarves to socks, knitwear offers warmth, comfort, and a variety of design possibilities. One of the defining features of knitwear is its use of stitches to create patterns and textures.
