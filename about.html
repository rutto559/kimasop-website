About

VISION

Our vision is only slightly loftier than sending a man to the moon. Knit Marketing exists to create quality jobs for untapped youth, while we grow our clients’ web presence through digital content marketing. We’ll equip hundreds of businesses with the tools they need while creating educational and career opportunities for thousands of teens and young adults.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to provide exceptional design services that exceed our clients' expectations, while fostering a culture of creativity, innovation, and sustainability. We strive to build long-term relationships with our clients, understanding their unique needs and delivering tailored solutions that enhance their lives.

Stitches and knit wear

SICD is looking forward to upgrade there products to meet our customers needs.

Knitwear refers to any type of clothing that has been knitted, which involves using needles and yarn to create fabric. The process of knitting results in a flexible, stretchy material that is ideal for creating cozy and functional garments. From sweaters to scarves to socks, knitwear offers warmth, comfort, and a variety of design possibilities. One of the defining features of knitwear is its use of stitches to create patterns and textures.
